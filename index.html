About Me

Hey, I'm Henry. I'm a St. John's University graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Healthcare Informatics. I'm a big fan of all things nerdy and tech, whether it's programming, building computers, fixing computers, gaming, and too many hobbies to list.

Coding is my passion. I enjoy hashing out code and finding solutions, and know the satisfaction of figuring out a difficult problem. My current focus is to succeed as a full stack web developer and develop scalable web applications which solve real world problems, big or small. You can see some examples of my work below.

Most of my larger projects utilize JavaScript and the MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) stack, but I'm also well-versed in several other languages and frameworks such as Java, SQL, and C++.
